HTML Entities & Symbols

HTML entities allow us to display special characters that might otherwise be interpreted as code.

1. Why Use HTML Entities?

  • Prevents conflicts with reserved characters (e.g., < and >).
  • Ensures special characters display correctly.
  • Supports mathematical symbols, currency symbols, and more.
